        You can't go wrong with chicken...but I was kind of getting paranoid because the chicken seemed undercooked. The bone was bright red the meat didn't feel firm.

        BACK A YARD
        This place is the real Caribbean deal! Located in downtown San Jose, they serve a delicious jerk chicken that is full of flavor and spice! Mildly spicy but super tasty and savory! I can eat this all day! Their combo plates also come with rice with black beans, a small salad, and plantains (who can say no to plantains).
